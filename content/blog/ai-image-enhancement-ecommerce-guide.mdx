## Common Image Problems E-commerce Sellers Face

### 1. Manufacturer-Supplied Images

Most manufacturers provide the bare minimum:
- Low resolution (often under 1000px)
- Inconsistent backgrounds
- Poor lighting
- No lifestyle context

### 2. Marketplace Requirements

Each platform has different standards:

**Amazon**
- Minimum 1000px on longest side (zoom requirement: 1600px+)
- Pure white background (RGB 255,255,255)
- Product fills 85% of frame

**Shopify**
- Recommended 2048×2048 for square images
- Consistent aspect ratios across catalog
- High-resolution for zoom

**eBay**
- Minimum 500px (recommended 1600px)
- No watermarks or borders
- Clear, well-lit photos

### 3. Multi-Channel Selling

Selling on multiple platforms means:
- Different size requirements for each
- Cropping and resizing constantly
- Maintaining quality across all versions

## Step-by-Step: Optimizing Product Images with AI

### Step 1: Audit Your Current Images

Before enhancing, identify what needs work:

**Resolution Check**
```
Image width × Image height = Total pixels
Minimum for e-commerce: 1,000,000 pixels (1 megapixel)
Ideal for zoom: 4,000,000+ pixels (4 megapixels)
```

**Quality Assessment**
- Are edges sharp or blurry?
- Is text readable when zoomed?
- Are colors accurate?
- Any visible compression artifacts?

### Step 2: Choose the Right Enhancement Level

| Source Quality | Recommended Upscale | Expected Result |
|---------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 500-800px | 2x | Good |
| 800-1200px | 2x | Excellent |
| 1200-1600px | 2x | Professional |
| Below 500px | 2x max | Acceptable |

<Callout type="warning">
Avoid upscaling more than 4x total. If your source is 400px and you need 3200px, quality will suffer. Consider sourcing better originals.
</Callout>

After AI enhancement, prepare platform-specific versions:

## Advanced Techniques for E-commerce Images

### Hero Image Optimization

Your main product image drives clicks. Maximize its impact:

1. **Start with the best angle**: Show the product's most appealing view

## Best Practices for E-commerce Image Management

### Maintain Original Files

Always keep your original, unprocessed images:
- AI and techniques improve over time
- Different platforms may need different processing
- Originals are your fallback

### Consistent Naming Convention

Organize enhanced images logically:
```
[SKU]_[angle]_[size].jpg
Example: PROD001_front_2048.jpg
```

### Quality Control Checklist

Before publishing, verify:
- [ ] Resolution meets platform minimum
- [ ] Text and labels are readable
- [ ] Colors appear accurate
- [ ] No visible artifacts or distortion
- [ ] Consistent with other catalog images
- [ ] File size optimized for web
