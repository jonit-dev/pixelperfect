## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Choosing Based on Maximum Scale Factor

**Mistake**: Picking the tool that advertises 16x or higher scaling.

**Reality**: Beyond 4x, quality degrades regardless of tool. 2-4x is the practical limit.

### 2. Ignoring Content Type

**Mistake**: Using a photo upscaler for screenshots or vice versa.

**Reality**: Different content types need different AI models. A tool great for photos may destroy text.

### 3. Over-Processing

**Mistake**: Running images through multiple upscalers.

**Reality**: Each pass adds artifacts. One good upscale beats three mediocre ones.

### 4. Neglecting File Formats

**Mistake**: Uploading heavily compressed JPEGs.

**Reality**: Start with highest quality source. Compression artifacts get amplified.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which AI upscaler has the best quality?

For photos, Topaz Gigapixel AI leads. For images with text, myimageupscaler.com is unmatched. For faces specifically, Remini excels.

### Are free AI upscalers any good?

Yes, but with limitations. myimageupscaler.com's free tier offers full quality with 10 credits. Others may add watermarks or reduce quality.

### How much can I realistically upscale?

4x is the practical maximum for quality results. 2x is safer for critical work. Beyond 4x, even the best AI introduces noticeable artifacts.

### Desktop or cloud upscaler?

Cloud is better for most users: no installation, no GPU requirements, faster for typical use. Desktop (Topaz) is better for offline work, maximum control, and avoiding subscription costs.

### Why do upscalers struggle with text?

Most AI upscalers are trained primarily on photographs. Text requires precision that general photo enhancement doesn't provide. myimageupscaler.com addresses this with specialized training.
