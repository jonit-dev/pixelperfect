### 2. Upscale.media

**Best for**: Quick, one-off upscaling tasks

**Free tier**: 3 images per day (no account required)

**Strengths**:
- No signup required for basic use
- Simple drag-and-drop interface
- Decent quality for photos
- Fast processing

**Limitations**:

### 3. Bigjpg

**Best for**: Anime and illustration upscaling

**Free tier**: 10 images per month

**Strengths**:
- Specialized anime/illustration model
- Preserves clean lines well
- Good for digital art
- Multiple model options

**Limitations**:
- Slow processing (often over 2 minutes)
- Poor results on photographs
- Text rendering is inconsistent
- Interface feels dated
- Limited monthly quota

**Our take**: If you're upscaling anime or digital illustrations, Bigjpg is worth trying. For photographs or anything with text, look elsewhere.

## What "Free" Really Means

Let's be honest about the true cost of "free" upscalers:

### Hidden Costs
- **Watermarks**: Many add logos you'll need to crop or pay to remove
- **Time limits**: Waiting in queues or daily/monthly caps
- **Quality restrictions**: Best results often locked behind paywalls
- **Data concerns**: Your images may be used for training or retained

### When Free Is Enough
- Personal projects
- Social media posts
- Testing before committing to a tool
- One-off, non-critical images

### When You Need Paid
- Professional/commercial use
- E-commerce product images
- High-volume processing
- Consistent, reliable quality
- Images with text or logos
