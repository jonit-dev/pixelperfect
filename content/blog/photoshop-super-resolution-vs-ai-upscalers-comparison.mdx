## What Is Photoshop Super Resolution?

Adobe's Super Resolution is an AI-powered upscaling feature integrated into Adobe Camera Raw (available in Photoshop and Lightroom). It uses machine learning trained on millions of image pairs to intelligently enlarge photos while adding detail.

### How It Works

Super Resolution uses Adobe's "Enhance Details" machine learning model to:
1. Analyze your image's content and patterns
2. Enlarge by exactly 2x resolution
3. Generate plausible detail based on learned patterns
4. Preserve edges and important structures

### Accessing Super Resolution

- **Photoshop**: Filter → Camera Raw → Select Photo → Photo Enhance → Super Resolution
- **Lightroom**: Photo → Enhance → Super Resolution
- **Lightroom Classic**: Photo → Enhance → Super Resolution

<Callout type="info">**Availability**: Super Resolution requires a Creative Cloud Photography plan (US$9.99/month) or higher. It's not available as a standalone purchase.</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Photoshop Super Resolution as good as standalone AI upscalers?

For general photography, quality is comparable. However, specialized AI upscalers excel at specific tasks like text preservation (MyImageUpscaler) or extreme upscaling (Topaz Gigapixel).

### Can I use Photoshop Super Resolution without a Creative Cloud subscription?

No. Super Resolution is only available with an active Creative Cloud Photography plan or higher. There's no standalone purchase option.

### Does Super Resolution work on JPEG files or only RAW?

Super Resolution works on JPEG, TIFF, and RAW files. However, results are generally better starting from RAW files due to the additional image data available.

### What's the maximum upscale factor in Photoshop Super Resolution?

Super Resolution only does 2x upscaling. For 4x or more, you'd need to run it multiple times (which degrades quality) or use a dedicated AI upscaler.

### Can I cancel Photoshop after using Super Resolution?

No. Your Creative Cloud subscription must remain active to access Photoshop and all its features. You can't use Super Resolution and then cancel.

### Which is better for batch processing?

Dedicated AI upscalers are much better for batch processing. MyImageUpscaler handles up to 10 images simultaneously, while Photoshop requires manual processing for each image.

### Does Super Resolution work offline?

Yes, once Photoshop is installed and activated, Super Resolution works completely offline. Web-based AI upscalers require an internet connection.

### What's the free option for upscaling without Photoshop?

MyImageUpscaler offers 10 free credits to test quality. Most web-based tools have some free tier, though with limitations compared to paid plans.
