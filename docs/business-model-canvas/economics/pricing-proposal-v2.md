# myimageupscaler.com Pricing Proposal v2.0

**Date:** December 2025
**Status:** Approved
**Cost Basis:** Real-ESRGAN via Replicate ($0.0017/run standard)

---

## Executive Summary

This pricing structure leverages Real-ESRGAN's exceptional cost efficiency ($0.0017/image) to:

1. Undercut competitors on entry pricing ($9 vs $12+)
2. Maintain 95%+ gross margins across all tiers
3. Offer generous free tier for viral growth
4. Position for premium tier expansion (future)

---

## Unit Economics

### Real-ESRGAN Cost Structure

| GPU             | Cost/Run | Speed | Use Case                 |
| --------------- | -------- | ----- | ------------------------ |
| T4 (Standard)   | $0.0017  | ~1.8s | Free, Starter, Pro       |
| A100 (Priority) | $0.0026  | ~0.7s | Business, priority queue |
| T4 + GFPGAN     | $0.0025  | ~2s   | Portrait enhancement     |

### Margin Analysis

```
At $0.0017/image (T4):
- Revenue per credit: $0.04-0.09 (depending on plan)
- Cost per credit: $0.0017
- Gross margin: 95-98%
```

## Key Pricing Decisions

### 1. No Watermarks on Free

- Viral growth driver
- Builds trust
- Differentiates from Let's Enhance, Pixelcut

### 2. Credit Rollover (2-6x by tier)

- Reduces churn
- Industry best practice (Let's Enhance success)
- Rollover multiplier increases with tier

### 3. Monthly Billing Default

- Lower barrier to entry
- Beats annual-only competitors (Upscale.media)
- Annual discount for cash flow optimization

### 4. Transparent $/Credit

- Builds trust
- Easy ROI calculation for users
- Counter to confusing credit systems (VanceAI)

## Pricing Psychology

### $9 Starter

- Under $10 = impulse purchase threshold
- "Less than 2 coffees/month"
- Lowest in market for 100 credits

### $29 Pro

- Sweet spot pricing
- 3.2x Starter = clear value jump
- Matches market mid-tier ($24-34)

### $99 Business

- Just under $100 psychological barrier
- Easy expense approval (<$100/mo)
- 3.4x Pro = encourages Pro for most
